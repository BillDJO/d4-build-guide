⚔️ Upheaval Barbarian

⚡ Skill Synergies

Bash → Upheaval: Bash generates Fury, Upheaval spends it for massive damage

War Cry + Rallying Cry: Buff damage and fury generation while providing team support

Wrath of the Berserker: Ultimate that amplifies all your damage and fury generation

💡 Tips for New Players

• Always use Bash to build Fury before casting Upheaval

• Keep your shouts active for constant buffs

• Use Upheaval on groups of enemies for maximum efficiency

• Focus on Two-Handed weapons for highest damage

💀 Blood Surge Necromancer

🎯 Build Focus

⚡ Skill Synergies

Decompose → Blood Surge: Decompose generates Essence and creates corpses for Blood Surge

Corpse Explosion + Blood Surge: Use corpses for both area damage and Blood Surge enhancement

Bone Armor + Blood Mist: Provides defense and escape when overwhelmed

💡 Tips for New Players

• Always maintain Bone Armor for protection

• Use Decompose to create corpses, then Blood Surge to consume them

• Corpse Explosion is great for clearing large groups

• Blood Mist is your panic button - use it to escape danger

• Focus on weapons with high Intelligence and +skill ranks
